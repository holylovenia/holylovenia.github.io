<div style="text-align: justify">While there have been a lot of studies on pornographic video detection, the majority of them focused in the image or video classification techniques. However, audio-based techniques are more efficient in terms of computational resources and number of features. Furthermore, sounds related to sexual activities (e.g., moans and groans) also incline to have different spectral characteristics than the sounds derived from non-pornographic videos.</div>
